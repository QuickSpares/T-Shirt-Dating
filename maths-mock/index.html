How this trainer works

  • Answer on screen — tap the number pad (no keyboard needed) or type. Use the ✍️ handwriting pad to work things out with your finger or Apple Pencil.
  • Check answer marks your response against the answer sheet.
  • If you're wrong, you get the full worked solution, the common mistakes for that question, and a method reminder.
  • At the end you see which topics you're strong in and which need work.
  • You get a study plan with links to BBC Bitesize and GOV.UK, saved to your training log so you can tick things off.
